Who Am I?

My name is Humza Lala, and I was born in Redmond, Washington. Growing up, I was surrounded by tech and constant innovation. I saw the rise of touch-screen phones and laptops, voice and facial recognition, smart-home technology, wireless accessories, and much more. All of these innovations facinated me, and I was contantly wondering how they worked. This facination led me to discover the field of tech and software and all the possibilites it enables. I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Washington, Seattle. I was accepted via the Robinson Center UW Academy Program, which is an early entrance program for high school students. I plan to major in Computer Science to pursue my passion for technology and innovation.
